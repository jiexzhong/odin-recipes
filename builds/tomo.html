Monokei Tomo

Description


The Tomo is a 75% kit featuring an aluminium bottom, brass weight and silver aluminium badges.
Designed by TGR, this board aims to be their first unlimited large scale keyboard group buy from TGR.

Specs

    TYPING ANGLE, WEIGHT & MOUNTING STYLE

  • Typing angle: 7 degrees
  • Weight: Standard Kit (1.6kg), SE Kit (2kg)
  • Mounting Style: Top mount
  • Dimensions: 31.5 x 13.5 x 3.5cm
  • Front height: 2cm
  • Firmware: PCB by Yiancar Designs.

Tomo Standard kit comes with:

  1. Solder / Hotswap PCB
  2. Set of aluminium silver anodised badges (MONOKEI & TGR)
  3. Polycarbonate (frosted) full plate
  4. Plate & case foam
  5. Aluminium silver anodised backplate
  6. Sandblasted brass weight
  7. Hex screws
  8. Rubber feet
  9. 1x O Ring

Mounting Style: Top & O-Ring

For this keyboard, there's not one but two mounting variations. The Gummy O-ring mount is a mounting style
that uses an O-ring as a gasket that sits between the plate and PCB. It utilizes specific PCB cutouts to
keep the plate in place. The O-ring gaskets help soften and reduce plate vibration allowing the keyboard
to have a lower pitch and soft feel. The Gummy O-ring mount is currently not as popular as other mounting
styles. However, with how successful keyboards such as the Singa Unikorn and Bakeneko60/65 are, enthusiasts
are now paying more attention to this mounting style. In this article, we will be taking a closer look at
