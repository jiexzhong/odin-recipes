Meridan R2

Description

A seamless design combined with sharp angles constructs a centerpiece fit for your modern desk.
The three LED indicators and identical alphanumeric bend angles carry on the legacy from the Prime_E. Designed by ai03.

Specs

  • PCB: Soldered, USB-C, ESD protected
  • Indicators: Physical LED indicators - RGB for initial batches; in-switch caps lock LED support
  • Plate: Aluminum, top mount
  • Typing Angle: 7 degrees
  • Alphanumerics Bend Angel: 8 degrees
  • Weight Brass, Interal, Bead Blasted

What's Included

  1. Solder PCB
  2. Aluminium Case
  3. Foams
  4. Full Aluminium Plate
  5. Half FR4 Plate
  6. Carrying Case

Mounting Style: Top Mount

Top Mount boards are among the most popular, securing the plate to the top housing of the keyboard.

My Personal Build (not pictured)

  • Switches: L&F Alpaca V2, 62g
  • Stabilizers: Durock V2, lubed with 205g
  • Case Foam Only

Thoughts

The 8 degrees rotation & split layout is pretty easy to adjust. Crispy sounding, while being on the stiffer side but I kinda dig it.
