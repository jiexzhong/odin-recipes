QK65

Description

Meet the QK65, the first keyboard from QwertyKeys. The QK65’s body has been designed from the ground up
to provide great value without sacrificing aesthetic or acoustic quality, featuring a CNC-machined aluminum
top case with anodized or electrophoretic finish, a PVD-coated stamped steel plate, a die-cast aluminum
bottom case, and a large molded silicone dampener that comfortably fills the space between.
Each QK65 kit comes with a solderable or hot-swappable ANSI PCB, as well as a plate made of your choice
of aluminum, FR-4, polycarbonate, or POM. All PCB and plate variations have unique flex cuts, and
includes two styles of gasket mounts alongside three pieces of case foam, allowing the QK65 to provide
a wide range of user-defined typing experiences.

Mounting Style: Gasket Mount

  • A gasket mount uses gasket material between the plate and keyboard housing on both top and bottom sides.
    This gives the board a slightly more cushioned feel since the plate isn’t in direct contact with the
    other metal components of the keyboard.
